The Winklevii: The Strong Guy(s) (Muscle)


Cameron and Tyler Winklevoss (hereon abbreviated to "The Winklevii") are the strong guys. Harvard jocks often depicted training for their inevitable Olympics race, The Winklevii are very punch first and ask questions later. Even though their approach to believing Mark took their concept and used it to create facebook, you can see the war waging inside of them, manifesting in one brother being cool and collected and the other wanting to come to blows. The muscle will always thing of the most direct most foreceful approach to their problems. Despite my belief that in certain versions of this film they may be considered charismatic or big picture thinkers, in this take written by Sorkin they lack all traits of a hero, and are actually oppositional to all other forces in the story.
