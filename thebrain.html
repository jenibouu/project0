Mark Zuckerberg: The Smart Guy (Brain)


Mark Zuckerberg is the protagonist of the film, to be sure, but that is not the same thing as a leader or hero. In this case, our protagnist is The Brain. We see it almost instantly in the speed with which he speaks to his date, the speed with which he answers questions in lecture, and then of course...the whole facebook thing. If ever there was a film with a character who exhibited advanced knowledge, this would be it. The trait he shares with The Leader is that he controls the action or inaction of the movie: can't have Facebook without Mark Zuckerberg, plain and simple. However, through the depiction in the film, the character lacks charisma and the unifiying big-picture thinking.
