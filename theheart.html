Eduardo Saverin: The "Chick" (Heart)


Eduardo Saverin is the only character who has a true emotional arc throughout the film. Unlike Mark, who is depicted as almost entirely emotionless, or Sean and the Winklevii whose feelings are, at most, negligible, Eduardo is the hopeful and loyal friend who gets betrayed in the end. Though the other characters offer more in the sense of utility and comedy, Eduardo is the character we pity. The only trait he shares in common with The Leader role is being a big-picture thinker, however he doesn't have the vision or charisma to actually make that a reality and therefore control the plot.
